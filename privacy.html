Privacy Policy

Your Privacy and Data Security

At HookFlow, we prioritize your privacy and the security of your data. This Privacy Policy details the information we collect and how we use it, specifically regarding natively recorded media.

1. Video and Audio Data

HookFlow does NOT harvest, upload, or sell your recorded video or audio data off-device natively. All cinematic recording, teleprompter generation, and data processing are performed securely. The contents of your video and audio remain strictly local to your device unless you explicitly share or export them to external platforms yourself.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect personally identifiable information unless directly provided by you (for example, when reaching out for support). We may collect anonymized, non-personal diagnostic data such as crash reports to improve the application's performance and stability, in accordance with Apple's standard analytics frameworks.

3. Subscriptions

All auto-renewable subscriptions and payments are handled securely by Apple via your iTunes Account. HookFlow does not store, process, or have access to your payment information or credit card numbers.

4. Third-Party Services

HookFlow utilizes native iOS frameworks (such as Speech Recognition and AVFoundation) that may send data to Apple for processing (e.g., Siri dictation servers), but this data is governed strictly by Apple's own Privacy Policy and is not retained or accessed by HookFlow.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy practices, please contact us at: support@hookflow.app
